Must-Visit Places

Taj Mahal, Agra

One of the Seven Wonders of the World, the Taj Mahal is a symbol of love and Mughal architecture.

Varanasi

Known as the spiritual capital of India, Varanasi is famous for its ghats, temples, and vibrant culture.

Lucknow

The city of Nawabs offers a rich heritage of Mughal and British architecture.

Ayodhya

The birthplace of Lord Rama, Ayodhya is steeped in history and spirituality.
